About

Welcome to Databased, the CS Crew at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc), where curiosity and innovation come together to create something extraordinary! We're a community of students passionate about diving deep into the world of computer science and related fields. We're here to provide a comprehensive resource for beginners to learn, grow, and create.


We organise weekly coding competitions, regular lectures on Machine Learning and Python from a compiler's perspective, as well as general discussions on coding. We also invite professors and industry leaders to deliver talks on various topics. One of our aims is to cultivate an entrepreneurial mindset within our members.


Databased is headed by Masavir Khaliq, an M.Tech. student at CSA, IISc, and is run by students of the B.Tech. (Maths and Computing) program. We are fortunate to have Chiranjib Bhattacharya (Former Chair, CSA) as our supervisor.
